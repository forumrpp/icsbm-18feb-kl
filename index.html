Introduction

Overview

The International Conference on Social Science, Business and Management Research (ICSBM 2024), scheduled for February 18, 2024, in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia (and ZOOM/Video), aims to provide a global platform for researchers, academics, and industry experts to exchange and discuss their latest research findings, ideas, and survey reports in both theoretical and practical aspects of Engineering and Technology. We invite prospective authors to submit their full papers or abstracts, which will undergo a rigorous blind review process conducted by at least two independent peers.

ICSBM 2024 serves as a catalyst for collaboration between academia and industry, fostering international networks. The conference offers significant opportunities for presentations and scholarly discussions. Attendees will benefit from a wide range of activities designed to bring together a diverse community of researchers and practitioners from various disciplines, fostering the generation of innovative ideas, potential partnerships, and business prospects. Professionals from industry, academia, government research and development, and professional practice in the field of Social Science, Business and Management are all warmly welcome to join ICSBM 2024.

Guidelines

Submission

In the first instance we require everyone who wishes to submit their work to the conference to submit an abstract describing the proposed paper, work in progress, presentation etc. The abstract submission form will guide you through the process but we recommend you read the call for papers first to ensure you select the correct track and submission type.

Abstract Submission: Please submit your abstract in English with a word limit of 150-350 words, aiming for an informative summary that includes background, purpose, methodology, results, and conclusion, and include a brief biography with your submission.

Full Paper Submission: Prospective authors are invited to submit full-text papers, including abstract, keywords, introduction, methodology, result description, tables, figures, and references; please ensure your paper is free of typographical and grammatical errors, especially if English is not your first language, as papers will be returned if the English quality is inadequate for publication; all submitted articles should present original, previously unpublished research results, whether experimental or theoretical, meeting the Conference's criteria and not being simultaneously considered for publication elsewhere, with manuscripts adhering to the Conference's style and undergoing review and editing.

Page Limit: In SCOPUS (Elsevier), Web of Science™ Core Collection, and ESCI journals, the standard article length is 7 pages, encompassing all elements such as figures, tables, and references, with additional pages incurring a charge, which exceeds 7 pages at a rate of 50 USD in Peer Review Journals.

Presentation Category:

  • Oral/Virtual Presentation: Opportunity to do a Oral/Live-Stream Presentation at the Conference.
  • E-poster Presentation: Electronic poster (e-poster) presentations are similar to traditional poster presentations but presented on a large computer screen.
  • Pre-Recorded Video Presentation: Authors who cannot attend the live session opt for presenting their work in a pre-recorded audio-visual format (.mp4) can avail of this option.

Fees & Payment
Registration Guidelines

After receiving the acceptance notice for your abstract, you can proceed to make your payment via Bank transfer/Online. Please be aware that payment is mandatory for each submission (title/abstract) and should be finalized prior to the Full Paper submission deadline.

Important Note:
  • To complete registration, make the payment and send us the payment receipt or transaction proof you received after the transaction you made along with the filled up registration form scanned copy before the last date of conference registration.
  • Delegates should bear the Service Charges (if any) of the bank on the Sender’s Side with registration fees.
  • Submit your Full paper for journal publication only after payment being confirmed by committee.
  • Registration fees you paid are non-refundable in case of not attending the conference or withdrawing the paper.
